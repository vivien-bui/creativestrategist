# Claude Design Prompt: Portfolio Direction

**Reference:** See `PORTFOLIO-DATA.md` in this folder for case study details, hex codes, and campaign stats.

---

## Role & Goal

You are an expert Brand Strategist and Senior Web Designer. Your task is to design a cohesive, highly personalized portfolio website for me by analyzing my personal aesthetic and suggesting how my five case studies could be presented.

## Provided Inputs

- **Instagram Screenshots (My Personality):** Two screenshots of my Instagram profile/feed showing my personal brand identity, visual taste, and aesthetic preferences.
- **Reference Screenshots (Inspiration):** Website layouts, structures, or UI elements I find compelling.

---

## Your Process

### Step 1: Extract My Vibe

Analyze my Instagram screenshots and tell me:
- **Overall Vibe & Tone:** What's the dominant feeling? (minimalist, moody, vibrant, playful, bold, ethereal, etc.)
- **Color Tendencies:** What colors, tones, and lighting dominate my aesthetic? Suggest 4–5 hex codes that capture this.
- **Typography Feeling:** What font energy matches my vibe? (serif vs sans, condensed vs airy, bold vs refined?)
- **Visual Motifs:** Any recurring shapes, themes, textures, or compositional styles?

### Step 2: Analyze the Reference Sites

Break down what makes the reference layouts work:
- Layout structure (grid, asymmetry, bento, full-bleed, etc.)
- Navigation approach
- How they organize and present content
- Interaction patterns (scroll behavior, hover states, transitions)

### Step 3: Suggest a Direction

Based on my personality (Step 1) and the reference sites (Step 2), propose **one cohesive design direction** for my portfolio. This should:
- Feel authentic to my aesthetic
- Work across my five case studies (which have wildly different color palettes — this is intentional complexity)
- Feel gallery-like, premium, and distinctive
- Give every case study equal presence regardless of how visually loud its imagery is

---

## Deliverables

Provide:

1. **Vibe Analysis:** What you see in my Instagram aesthetic (tone, colors, typography, motifs)
2. **Design Direction:** A clear, single direction you're recommending with rationale
3. **System Overview:** 
   - Color philosophy (how to unify or separate the five case studies)
   - Typography approach
   - Key UI principles (glass? brutalism? editorial? kinetic? etc.)
4. **Homepage Blueprint:** A descriptive walkthrough of how the homepage would feel and function
5. **Code Skeleton:** HTML + CSS (or React) for the Hero section showing your suggested direction
6. **Visual Mockup:** Screenshot or detailed description of how the Hero/key sections would look

---

## Context Notes

- My five case studies have very different color palettes, so your system needs to handle visual range without letting every palette scream at once
- I'm a creative strategist, so the portfolio should reflect strategic thinking, not just pretty visuals
- This is for agency contract work, so it should feel premium and professionally distinctive
- I want genuine creative exploration from you—suggest what you think works best, not just what I asked for
